# InsureSense AI — Insurance AI Operations Hub

**Intelligent Claims Processing. Fraud Detection. Customer Experience. One Platform.**

---

## The Problem

Insurance operations teams are overwhelmed. Claims backlogs grow while adjusters manually review documents, flag fraud by gut instinct, and handle customer inquiries without real-time AI support. Every delayed claim costs money, erodes trust, and increases regulatory risk.

## The Solution

**InsureSense AI** is a production-ready operations hub that brings nine specialized AI agents together to triage claims, detect fraud, answer customer questions, and extract intelligence from documents — all in under 60 seconds, with built-in compliance safeguards.

---

## What It Does

| Capability | Business Value |
|---|---|
| **Claims Triage** | Submit a claim with text and evidence. AI agents assess severity (Critical / High / Medium / Low), assign urgency, and recommend next actions — reducing first-touch processing time from hours to seconds. |
| **Fraud Detection & Scoring** | Every claim receives a 0-100 risk score across five categories: timing anomalies, behavioral patterns, financial irregularities, document inconsistencies, and historical red flags. High-risk claims are automatically flagged for your Special Investigations Unit. |
| **Cross-Claim Fraud Correlation** | Four detection strategies — date proximity, narrative similarity, shared risk flags, and severity matching — surface fraud rings and repeat offenders across your entire claims portfolio, with claim-type-specific analysis windows. |
| **CX Copilot** | A real-time streaming chat assistant for customer-facing teams. Answers policyholder questions with tone-aware responses, automatic escalation detection, and regulatory disclaimers — so agents stay compliant without slowing down. |
| **Document Intelligence** | Upload policy documents, adjuster notes, or evidence files. The system extracts text, indexes it semantically, and lets you search with natural language queries — returning answers with precise citations back to source documents. |
| **Provider Health Monitor** | A live dashboard showing the status of every AI service in the chain, so your ops team always knows the system is running and where to look if it is not. |

## Why InsureSense AI

**Resilient by Design** — Not one AI provider, but seven, organized in an automatic fallback chain. If any provider goes down or hits a rate limit, the system switches to the next in milliseconds. Zero single points of failure.

**Compliance-First Architecture** — Personal information (SSNs, policy numbers, phone numbers, emails) is redacted *before* any data leaves your environment for AI processing. Dual-pass redaction on every customer interaction. Your data stays yours.

**Zero Operational Cost** — The entire platform runs on free-tier cloud services with hard spending caps that can never generate a surprise bill. Scale into paid tiers only when your volume justifies it.

**Tested and Proven** — 1,053 automated tests across backend, frontend, and end-to-end scenarios with zero failures. Accessibility audited to WCAG AA standards.

**Modular and Extensible** — Orchestration profiles activate only the agents each task needs, saving 50-60% on processing overhead. Add new AI capabilities without rearchitecting.
